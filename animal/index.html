Description

Buttons

  • Stand up: Make the cat stand up
  • Sit down: Make the cat sit down
  • Reset: Reset the cat to initial status
  • Show Debug Options: Show more options for debuging

Effects

  • It will have a slight sway when standing
  • You can click it's body to make it sit down or stand up
  • You can click it's legs to make it stretch or stand up
  • You can click on a canvas point away from the animal to make it move to that point
  • You can click it's head to make it fell happy
  • You can click it's tail to make it Wag it's tail

Debugs

  • Show Click Box: Draw click box area for every part of the cat
  • Fill Click Box: Fill click box area for every part of the cat
  • Show Bone: Draw the "bones" of the cat
  • Print States: Print out the cat's states in the browser console
